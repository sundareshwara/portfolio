Experimental Study on Bricks using Iron Ore Tailings and Waste Foundry Sand

This project involved a comprehensive research study to explore the effects of using iron ore tailings (IOT) and waste foundry sand (WFS) as partial replacements for cement and M sand in brick production. A total of 15 different trials were conducted, each producing three bricks with varying replacement ratios.
The trial mix T1, containing 30% IOT and 10% WFS, showed the highest compressive strength, reaching up to 70.44% of that of conventional bricks
